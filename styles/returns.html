Returns Policy

  • Eligibility for Return

    Vehicles can be returned within 14 days of purchase, provided they have not exceeded 200 miles and are in the same condition as at the time of purchase.

  • Return Process

    To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team with your purchase details and reason for return. A representative will guide you through the process.

  • Restocking Fee

    A restocking fee of 10% of the vehicle's purchase price will be applied for all returns.
